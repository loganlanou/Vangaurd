Automated Tests

Manual Verification Steps

Step 1: Open VANGUARD Studio

Navigate to: http://localhost:3000/studio

Step 2: Check for Accent/Price Color Control

1. Click on the "Colors" tab in the left panel

2. Scroll to "Text Colors" section

3. Look for a new color picker labeled "Accent/Price"

Step 3: Navigate to Pricing Page

1. In the preview panel, click "Pricing" in the navigation menu

2. You should see pricing cards with prices: $9/mo, $29/mo, Custom

Step 4: Test Color Change

1. Change the "Accent/Price" color picker to a bright color (e.g., red, green, or yellow)

2. Verify that the pricing text ($9/mo, $29/mo, Custom) changes color immediately

3. Try different colors to confirm the control works
